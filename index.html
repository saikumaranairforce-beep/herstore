Key Characteristics of Women Entrepreneurs in Tamil Nadu Growth: Tamil Nadu is one of the states with a high number of women entrepreneurs, showing steady growth in women-led businesses. Major Sectors: Women entrepreneurs are actively involved in sectors such as food products, handicrafts, fashion and apparel design, beauty salons, and information technology start-ups. Support Systems: Government organizations like TAHDCO and TN-RISE Women Start Up Council provide support through loan facilities, training programs, and marketing assistance. Challenges: Despite their progress, women entrepreneurs still face challenges such as raising finance, marketing their products, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance.
